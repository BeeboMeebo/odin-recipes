Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf! Beethoven called it the dish of the gods. Inspired by the peat bogs in Western Europe, primeval man used to live on this stuff. You know what most historians say: "Don't follow economics, don't follow religion, don't follow power. Follow meatloaf. Its about as delicious as it gets, while also being protein-rich.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Fukin KETCHUP

Steps

  1. Chop and caramelize onions
  2. Combine all ingredients in bowl
  3. Massage
  4. Form a loaf on a baking sheet
  5. Bake (Cooking times vary)
  6. Cover with sauce
  7. Bake a lil more
  8. Enjoy!
