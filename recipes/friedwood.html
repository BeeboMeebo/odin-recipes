Fried Wood

Description

A family classic! Featured by Bahl "Health-man" Sachs in WonderFoods Daily. Fried wood is a deep fried twist on one of the most fibrous foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • Fresh softwood log
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, milk, and breadcrumbs in bowl
  2. Cut wood into appoprately sized blocks
  3. Baste in fry shit
  4. Fry it!
  5. Enjoy!
